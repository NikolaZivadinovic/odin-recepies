Burger

Burger is the besto drunk junkk food to ever be invented!

Now we will talk about what you will need, and which steps you should take to make yourself this marvel of junk food!

Ingridents

  • Burger bun
  • Minced meat (beef or pork or 50%/50% mix) about 250gr per person
  • Burger sauce that you like
  • Lettuce of your choosing
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chilli sauce

Steps

Here are some guidlines you should follow to assemble this marvel.

  1. Slice the bun in half horizontally.
  2. Put your sauces on the bun as you like.
  3. Fry your meat to your liking.
  4. Once finished put your meat in the bun.
  5. Not 'your meat' dumbass, the burger!
  6. Put some lettuce on it if you want to.
  7. Put sauces you like the best.
  8. Be messy as a pig and enjoy!
