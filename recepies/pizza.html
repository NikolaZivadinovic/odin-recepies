Steps

Here are some steps you can take to make a delicious pizza.

  1. Make pizza dough a night prior
  2. Let the dough rest for at least 12hrs
  3. Strech the dough to fit the pan.
  4. Cover the pizza dough with homemade pizza sauce.
  5. Put first layer of cheese.
  6. Put the rest of the ingridents.
  7. Cover with shreded cheese.
  8. Put it in oven on high heat 220 degrees Celsius.
  9. Enjoy your pizza!
